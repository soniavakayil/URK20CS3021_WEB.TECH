Dark Academia

Dark academia romanticises a time when the upper classes of society put an emphasis on liberal education. An interest in classical literature, languages such as Latin, Greek or French, architecture, art, poetry and fashion from Western Europe characterise the subculture.

Light Academia

Light Academia themes are generally positive, focusing on optimism, sensitivity, joy, gratitude, friendship, motivation, and happy endings. Visuals from classical antiquity still play a significant role in Light Academia, and the Western canon are a significant part of the philosophy.
